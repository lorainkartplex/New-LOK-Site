Black River Kart Club

Northeast Ohio's premier kart racing club — compete on a world-class 0.82-mile circuit at Lorain Ohio KartPlex

↻ Drag track to rotate
The Circuit

World-Class Track

Black River Kart Club (BRKC) is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization and the competitive kart racing arm of Lorain Ohio KartPlex. Founded in 2025, BRKC is built around a fair, organized, and competitive environment for drivers of all ages and experience levels.

  • 0.82-mile international competition circuit
  • 18 technical turns
  • 850-foot main straightaway
  • 37.5-acre facility
