Essentials

What You Need to Race

Every racer needs safety gear and equipment to get on track

Helmet

Snell SA2020 or newer certified karting or auto racing helmet required for all drivers.

Racing Suit

A karting suit (CIK/FIA rated) is required. One-piece or two-piece suits are accepted.

Gloves & Shoes

Karting gloves for grip and closed-toe shoes. Racing-specific shoes recommended.

Rib Protector

Highly recommended for all drivers, especially in higher-speed 2-stroke classes.

Kart & Engine

A race-ready chassis and engine. Use our Kart Finder above to find the right setup.

Club Membership

A Black River Kart Club membership gives you discounted race entry fees and championship eligibility.
