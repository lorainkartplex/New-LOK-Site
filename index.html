About the Facility

Ohio's Premier
Karting Destination

Lorain Ohio KartPlex is a 37.5-acre world-class motorsports entertainment complex on the shores of Lake Erie. Featuring a 0.85-mile international competition circuit with 15 feet of elevation change, a dedicated 0.43-mile rental circuit, indoor electric karting, professional racing simulators, and a full-service dealership and pro shop — there's something for everyone from first-time drivers to championship-caliber racers.

The KartPlex is home to Black River Kart Club (BRKC), Northeast Ohio's premier competitive karting club, as well as Apex Sim Racing Arena, KartZone dealership, Ohio Kart Parts, and Collision Bend Brewery.

  • ■ 0.85-mile competition track with 18 turns
  • ■ 0.43-mile rental circuit for all ages
  • ■ Indoor electric karting & sim racing
  • ■ Full-service dealership, pro shop & brewery
  • ■ Heated garages & kart storage
Our Journey

Building the KartPlex

2024
The Vision Takes Shape
Land acquired at 2000 Root Road in Lorain, Ohio. Track design and engineering begin for what will become Ohio's premier karting destination.
2025
Track Construction & BRKC Launch
Competition track construction completed. Black River Kart Club (BRKC) launches its inaugural race season in July 2025 with 18 competitive classes.
Spring 2026
Grand Opening
Full facility grand opening — rental circuits, indoor electric karting, Apex Sim Racing Arena, Ohio Kart Parts pro shop, and Collision Bend Brewery all open to the public.
Beyond
Growing the Experience
Continued expansion with new race series, league competitions, national events, and community programming. Building Northeast Ohio's motorsports future.

Black River Kart Club

BRKC is the competitive racing arm of Lorain Ohio KartPlex. With 18 race classes across Rotax, IAME, Tillotson, and Briggs 206 platforms, plus a 14-race championship season — BRKC is where serious kart racers compete. Registration is open now.
