On-Site Facilities

The Complex

37.5 Acres of Motorsports Entertainment

A one-of-a-kind destination with everything under one roof — racing, rentals, simulators, events, dining, and more. The complex is currently under active construction and will be opening in phases. Explore the facilities below to see what's coming and when to expect each one to open.

Black River Kart Club

March 2026

Your ultimate destination for high-speed competition on our 0.85-mile international circuit — 18 technical turns, an 850-foot straightaway, and world-class racing conditions for professional kart drivers and kart owners.

Learn More

KartZone

March 2026

A premier karting dealership and full-service center with a retail store featuring gear and apparel, parts department, and complete kart packages.

Learn More

Kart Garages & Rack Storage

Available Now

Keep your kart trackside with single or double heated garage units, plus convenient in-and-out single rack storage options.

Learn More

Apex Karting Academy

April 2026

A driver development program featuring competitive karting camps for beginners to experienced pros — build your skills on a world-class circuit.

Learn More

Outdoor Rental Karting

May 2026

Hit the 0.43-mile outdoor rental circuit — designed for all ages and skill levels. No experience needed, just show up and drive.

Learn More

Apex Sim Racing Arena

Summer 2026

Professional-grade racing simulators where you can compete on world-famous circuits — leagues, walk-ins, and private events.

Corporate Event Center & Party Rooms
