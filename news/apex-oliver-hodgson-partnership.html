Apex Karting Academy Announces Oliver Hodgson as First Pro Karting Partner and Launches Competition Karting Camps

Lorain, OH — Apex Karting Academy, based at the Lorain Ohio KartPlex in Lorain, Ohio, is excited to announce that professional kart racer and coach Oliver Hodgson has joined the program as its first Pro Karting Partner. This partnership also marks the official launch of Apex Karting Academy’s Competition Karting Camps, scheduled to take place April 2–4 and April 6–7 at the Lorain Ohio Kartplex.

Oliver Hodgson brings world-class credentials to Apex Karting Academy. Now 32, Hodgson is a highly accomplished professional karter, highlighted by his Rotax Grand Finals victory in 2013 and an exceptional streak of success at the Rotax US Trophy Finals, where he has won championships for the past four consecutive years. Beyond his success on track, Hodgson is a proven karting coach who has helped numerous drivers improve their race craft, confidence, and on-track results.

As the Academy’s initial professional partner, Hodgson will play a central role in the Competition Karting Camps, offering structured group training for drivers 8+ and have competition kart packages available for rent to help avoid transportation costs. These group sessions are recommended for drivers with prior racing experience who are looking to accelerate their development in a competitive training environment. In addition, Apex Karting Academy will offer one-to-one half-day coaching sessions with Hodgson, available to drivers ranging from beginners to elite-level competitors.

“This partnership is a huge step forward for Apex Karting Academy,” said Kristopher McCrone, Founder of the Apex Karting Academy. “Bringing someone of Oliver’s experience, championship pedigree, and coaching ability to the Lorain Ohio KartPlex allows us to offer a level of driver development that is rarely available at the regional level. We’re proud to have him as our first Pro Karting Partner.”

Hodgson expressed his enthusiasm for the partnership, stating, “I’m really excited to partner with Apex Karting Academy and be part of what they’re building at the Lorain Ohio Kartplex. My goal is to help drivers understand not just how to go fast, but why they’re going fast, and to give them the tools they need to succeed at the highest level of karting. The Competition Karting Camps on April 2–4 and April 6–7 are a great opportunity for drivers who are serious about improving.”

The partnership between Apex Karting Academy and Oliver Hodgson represents a major commitment to high-level karting education in the Midwest and further establishes the Lorain Ohio KartPlex as a destination for competitive driver development.

For pricing, availability, and general information on the Apex Karting Academy, Competition Karting Camps, please contact us at info@apexkartingacademy.com

About Apex Karting Academy

Apex Karting Academy is a driver development program based at the Lorain Ohio KartPlex in Lorain, Ohio, dedicated to helping kart racers of all ages and experience levels reach their full potential through professional coaching and structured training programs.
