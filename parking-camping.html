Parking & Camping

Everything you need to know about parking and overnight stays at the KartPlex

Parking at the KartPlex

Lorain Ohio KartPlex offers free parking for all visitors. Our paved parking lots are located just steps from the main entrance, spectator areas, and the Collision Bend Brewery taproom. Whether you're here for a race weekend, a group event, or just stopping by to watch the action, there's plenty of space.

General Parking

Free paved parking close to the main entrance with easy access to spectator areas, the taproom, and all facilities. Accessible spaces and overflow parking available for large events.

Free Parking for Regular Vehicles

RV & Trailer Parking

Designated areas for RVs, motorhomes, and kart trailers. Stay on-site during race weekends and multi-day events with easy access to the paddock and track. Overnight parking and camping is available during Black River Kart Club and other race series events, with overnight security provided on race weekends.
