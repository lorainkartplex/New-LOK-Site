About BRKC

Built for Serious
Kart Racers

Black River Kart Club (BRKC) is the competitive karting arm of the Lorain Ohio KartPlex — a world-class motorsports destination on the shores of Lake Erie. We exist for the dedicated racer: the driver who owns their own machine, obsesses over setup, and lives for the checkered flag.

The facility features a 0.82-mile international competition circuit with 18 technical turns, on-site kart storage, heated garages, and a full-service dealership. Our race program launched in July 2025 and is built around a fair, organized, and competitive environment for drivers of all age groups.
